About Hydrolife Clinic

Your Wellness,
Our Priority

Hydrolife Clinic is a private medical and aesthetic practice founded by Kamogelo Molokomme, a registered nurse, midwife, and qualified beauty therapist. We combine evidence-based healthcare with cutting-edge aesthetic treatments – all under one roof.

Located at 69 Pretorius Street, JNA Centre Complex in the heart of Mokopane, we're fully licensed by the South African Nursing Council (SANC) and dedicated to the highest standards of safety and personalised care.

Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked
Questions

Do I need to book an appointment?
Yes, we operate on an appointment-only basis to ensure dedicated attention and minimal waiting time. You can book via WhatsApp, our contact form, or by phone.
Are your practitioners qualified and registered?
Absolutely. Our practitioner holds a Diploma in Nursing (General, Community, Psychiatric, and Midwifery), certification in IV Vitamin Therapy, and Beauty Therapy qualifications. Fully registered with SANC and licensed for independent practice.
What should I bring to my first visit?
Please bring your ID, medical aid card (if applicable), and a list of any current medications. Your first visit includes a thorough consultation and personalised care plan.
Do you accept medical aid?
We are a private clinic. Please contact us directly to discuss payment options and flexible payment arrangements.
What are your operating hours?
We're open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and Saturdays from 8:00 AM to 2:00 PM. Closed on Sundays but available by special appointment on public holidays.
